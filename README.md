# Business-Performance-Dashboard
📊 Business Performance Dashboard | Excel Project

An interactive, fully dynamic Excel Dashboard built to track and analyze core business KPIs — Subscribers, Leads, Revenue, Deals, Traffic Sources, and Regional Coverage — across a 4-year period (2025–2028), using pivot tables, Power Query, and dynamic charting instead of static reporting.


🎯 Project Goal

Turn a raw monthly business dataset into a single, interactive dashboard that lets stakeholders filter, drill down, and monitor performance live — without touching the underlying data.


📁 Dataset Overview

The underlying dataset (Data sheet) contains 48 monthly records spanning 2025 to 2028, with the following fields:

CategoryFieldsTimeYear, MonthFinancialsRevenue, Expenses, Sales TargetSubscriptionsStandard, Premium, Subscribers, Subscribers TargetFunnelLeads, DealsTraffic SourcesReferral, Social, Organic, Other (%)Regional CoverageN, NE, E, SE, S, SW, W, NW, Coverage Target


📈 Key Insights from the Data


Revenue grew from $525K in 2025 to $1.34M in 2028, a ~2.6x increase over 4 years.
Subscribers grew from 526 (end of 2025) to 2,000 (end of 2028), nearly 4x growth.
Overall funnel conversion rate (Deals ÷ Leads) across all years stands at ~63.5%.
Expenses scaled faster in later years (from $320K in 2025 to $1.28M in 2028), narrowing the profit margin as the business expanded — a key point highlighted on the dashboard's Profit trendline.
Regional coverage expanded steadily year over year, moving from partial coverage in the North/Northeast in 2025 to full (100%) coverage across all 8 regions by 2028.



📊 Dashboard Highlights

✅ KPI Overview Cards — Total Subscribers, Total Leads, Revenue (vs. Target), Deals (with Conversion Rate)
✅ Traffic Sources Donut Chart — Referral, Social, Organic, and Other, with an overall performance ring (69%)
✅ Monthly Trend Chart — Toggleable Expenses / Profit / Revenue lines with a Sales Target reference line
✅ Subscriber Growth Chart — Year-over-year bars (2025 → 2028)
✅ Coverage Radar Chart — Regional performance across 8 directions (N, NE, E, SE, S, SW, W, NW) vs. target
✅ Interactive Filters — Month buttons, year slicers, and a toggle switch for live filtering

💡 Project Workflow


Requirement Gathering — Defined the core KPIs the business needed to monitor (growth, funnel efficiency, traffic quality, regional coverage).
Data Preparation — Structured raw monthly data into a clean, single source-of-truth table.
Data Modeling — Built pivot tables to aggregate Revenue, Expenses, Subscribers, Leads, and Deals by month/year.
KPI Calculations — Created formulas for conversion rate, revenue-to-target %, and subscriber growth.
Interactive Dashboard Design — Combined dynamic charts, slicers, and a toggle switch into a single dashboard view.
Insight Generation — Surfaced growth trends, funnel performance, and coverage gaps at a glance.
